About Our Group

About us: We’re a small co-ed lacrosse group made mostly of casual players who played in high school. We’re always open to new players, and we will provide equipment for beginners. We meet every Saturday at 9 AM -12 PM on the Upper Playfields 5 and 6, by the ropes course, or indoors when it’s too cold.
